About Hermel

The Hermel District is a district in the Baalbek-Hermel Governorate of Lebanon. Its semi-arid land contributes to its low population density. It borders the Akkar District and Miniyeh-Danniyeh District on its west, the Baalbek District in the south and east, and Syria on its north. The capital of the Hermel District is Hermel.
