About Nabatiyeh

The Nabatieh District is a district in the Nabatieh Governorate of Lebanon. The capital of the district is Nabatieh. The town's name originates with the Phoenician word meaning . Nabatieh is known for its grottos, the most important of which are the grottos of Ich Al-Ghourab, and Maghar Mahalah, the red spot. Also of interest are some ancient rock tombs, Roman and Byzantine ruins, and two very old mosques.
