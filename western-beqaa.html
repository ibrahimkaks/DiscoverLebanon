About Western-Beqaa

Western Beqaa District is an administrative district in the Beqaa Governorate of the Republic of Lebanon. The capital is Joub Jannine.

Area of Western-Beqaa

Western-Beqaa covers an area of 425 km2

Population

Population of Western-Beqaa is about 300,000
