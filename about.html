Launched in 2022, Discover Lebanon navigation website started as a summative learnig course project by two computer and communication engineering students at Rafic Hariri University: Aline Hassan and Ibrahim Kaakour.



The purpose of this webpage is to let people discover Lebanon and its districts.



Moreover, other than providing the map of Lebanon, this webpage can help also in lebanese elctions since it provides information that can be used in electoral statistics.



Therefore, this webpage is useful for both tourism and elections.
