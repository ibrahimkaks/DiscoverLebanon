About Tyre

Tyre (Sour) District is one of three districts of South Lebanon Governorate. Its administrative center is locate in the historic city of Tyr (Sour), one of the oldest coastal cities of the Mediterranean Basin. Tyre (Sour) has a sandy coastline considered one of the most beautiful and largest of Lebanon. Tyre (Sour) District is bounded on the north by Saida (Sidon) District, on the east by Bent Jbayl District and on the South by the international border.
